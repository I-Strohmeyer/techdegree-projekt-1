Hello, I’m Herbert.

My home is the Bermuda Triangle.

Background

I’m a weary traveler that has seen a lot, one could almost say I have seen too much.

Imagine seeing your friends struggle for their life, because they have either eaten something that is now commonly found floating in the ocean, and if it's not that, then they get strangled by plastic.

I am not able to tell you how old I am exactly, but you can assume that I have been here for a while. Getting older doesn't make it exactly easier seeing the same thing happen again and again, not to mention that it's getting worse and worse from year to year. Most humans have no idea what we have to deal with and how it's affecting us all. Do they even realise that this is going to catch up to them one way or another? By that time it will be too late for many of my friends.

Goals

In order to help those that are dear to me (including myself) I wish to gain knowledge.

I want to master the process of building web sites and increase my knowledge, skills and abilities in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Angular
  • Node.js

I’d like to work for a non-profit organization that is interested and strives to rid our ocean of all the trash that is killing my friends.

But the first step is to raise awareness for this issue and I have confidence that once I have a better idea how this world wide web works and how exactly humans use it to share, exchange and generate content, I will do my part and educate the humans living in this world, our only world.
